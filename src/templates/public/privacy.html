Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Meo GPT Connector (the "Connector") handles personal data when you use the ChatGPT integration for Meo Mai Moi pet care services.

1. What this Connector does

The Connector allows ChatGPT to securely call Meo Mai Moi APIs on your behalf after you authorize access. The Connector acts as a technical bridge and does not replace the core Meo Mai Moi application.

2. Data we process

The Connector may process:

  • Account identifiers needed to authenticate and authorize API requests.
  • Pet profile data and health records that you request through ChatGPT tools.
  • Technical request metadata such as endpoint path, response status, latency, and request IDs.

3. How data is used

We process data only to:

  • Authenticate you and issue short-lived access for ChatGPT actions.
  • Execute your requested pet-management actions.
  • Protect service security, rate-limit abuse, and debug operational issues.

4. Authentication and tokens

The Connector uses OAuth 2.0 Authorization Code flow and issues signed JWTs for ChatGPT. Tokens from the main application are encrypted within JWT payloads and are not intentionally logged in plaintext.

5. Logging and security

The Connector is designed to avoid logging sensitive token values. Operational logs are limited to technical metadata required for reliability and security monitoring.

6. Data sharing

Data is shared with Meo Mai Moi systems strictly to fulfill your requests. We do not sell personal data. Third-party processors may be used for hosting, storage, and infrastructure operations.

7. Data retention

Data retention is determined by Meo Mai Moi operational and legal requirements. Connector-specific transient session data (for example, OAuth state and authorization codes) is stored temporarily and expires quickly.

8. Your choices and rights

You may revoke Connector access at any time using the integration controls or by contacting Meo Mai Moi support. Depending on your jurisdiction, you may also have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data.

9. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, subject to appropriate safeguards where required by applicable law.

10. Children

This service is intended for users who can lawfully manage their own accounts. If you believe personal data was provided by a child without proper authorization, contact support to request review.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the Last updated date above.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact Meo Mai Moi support through the official support channels of the main application.
